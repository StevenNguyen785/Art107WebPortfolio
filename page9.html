Title: Gym Life Shenanigans: Episode 3 Prologue and Full Length Episode

Dimension: 1920x1080 screen resolution

Medium: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro, and Adobe After Effects

Brief Artist Statement About The Projects: (SPOILER WARNING AHEAD ABOUT THE VIDEOS ABOVE!) This is the full length and prologue of Episode 3 from Gym Life Shenanigans. These are 2 different videos with 2 different narratives. In order for my series to be considered full length, it has to be more than 3 minutes. Episode 1 which is the pilot is roughly 2 minutes so I don't consider that full length compared to Episode 2 & 3. The pilot is mainly just a test video to see if I enjoyed using After Effects or not which in this case I really like using it. Full length Episode 3 is a continuation from Episode 3: Prologue in terms of the story. The prologue is a conintuation from Episode 2. The synopsis of the prologue is about Former Buff Guy had enagaged a relationship with Pretty Gym Girl. Full Length Episode 3 is about how Former Buff Guy is grieving after the break up by his former lover which is Pretty Gym Girl. Episode 4 is currently being developed and will be the first season finale. As usual, the tools that are being used to make Episode 4 are Adobe After Effects, Photoshop, and Premiere Pro which are the same ones I've used in the previous Episodes. I don't know if or when I start making the second season, but I am considering developing a brand new series using the same tools I used for the Gym Life Shenanigan series.

Research Details: I have integrated humor from other TV Shows and sitcom into the series. The techniques that I learned from using different Adobe softwares are from various YouTube tutorials. The website, hiclipart.com is a good source to find almost any png images for a project.
